About Us

Our Mission

আমাদের লক্ষ্য হল আপনাকে আপনার প্রাপ্য সঠিক তথ্য দেওয়া এবং এক শাখা অন্য শাখার সাথে যোগাযোগ শক্ত করা আমরা যাতে মিলেমিশে থাকতে পারি এবং একে অপরের সহযোগিতা করি। এই ওয়েবসাইটে আমরা আমাদের ক্লাস সম্পর্কে সব ধরনের তথ্য যোগ করার চেষ্টা করেছি। কিন্তু যদি আপনি কিছু অনুপস্থিত বা কোন সমস্যা খুঁজে পান তাহলে আমাদের সাথে যোগাযোগ করুন। সব শেষে আমরাও মানুষ।

Our History

Once upon a time, there lived a man who was weak, both in body and spirit. He often found himself on the losing side of life, beaten down by those stronger than him. But one day, after being brutally attacked, something inside him snapped. He decided that he would never be helpless again. He trained relentlessly, honing his physical strength and mastering his fighting skills. His arms became powerful weapons, and for a while, he felt invincible.

But as time passed, he realized that raw strength wasn't everything. There was a void inside him that brute force couldn't fill. So, he abandoned the path of violence and turned inward, seeking power within his mind. He delved deep into his thoughts, sharpening his intellect and expanding his understanding of the world. He became wise, capable of outthinking any opponent.

Yet, despite his newfound strength and wisdom, there was a darkness that lingered within him. He began to feel a strange, unsettling emotion—a pull toward the shadows, where he could use his skills unseen, without recognition or praise. He became a ghost in the dark, performing acts of great skill and bravery, but never stepping into the light to claim credit.

This hidden life brought him a peculiar kind of happiness, a thrill that made his heart race as if he were on the edge of life and death. It was a secret joy, one that no one else could understand. And even now, that feeling, that rush, keeps him tied to the darkness. He loves it, embraces it, and would never trade it for anything else.
